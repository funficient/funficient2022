Activities & Experiences

Picture productive workdays in a peaceful environment, mid-day walks to the lake or forest labyrinth, evenings enjoying unobstucted views of the star filled sky, and weekends exploring South Africa’s most majestic landscapes.

Enjoy Scenic Hiking Trails & San Cave Art at Giant's Castle

Giant’s Castle Nature Reserve, a UNESCO world heritage site, is celebrated for its breathtaking landscapes and ancient cultural heritage.

Visit the San rock art centre where paintings created by South Africa’s earliest hunter-gatherers depict spiritual rituals, animals, and daily life; hike to dramatic escarpment viewpoints, and spot endangered bearded vultures soaring above the cliffs.

Safari Off The Beaten Track at Weenen Game Reserve

Set in the rolling savannah, Weenen Game Reserve is a conservation success story where farmland has been restored to thriving wilderness.

Embark on game drives or walking safaris to see white and black rhino, giraffe, zebra, antelope, and abundant birdlife in their natural habitat. With its peaceful landscapes and acacia woodlands every turn offers the chance to witness wildlife up close without the crowds of larger reserves.

Read More

Go Flyfishing in The Midlands Meander

Winding through rolling hills and valleys from Mooirivier to Hilton, the Midlands Meander offers 80km of a variety of uniquely South African art, culture, and nature.

Explore artisan studios, craft breweries and markets where artists, potters, cheesemakers, and weavers share their passion for sustainable, handmade living. Between stops, visit the Nelson Mandela capturing site, go flyfishing, or simply soak up the tranquility of the green landscapes dotted with waterfalls and forests.

Enjoy Wine Tastings and Craft Beer Experiences

In the heart of the countryside, Abingdon Wine Estate stands out as KwaZulu-Natal’s first family-run boutique wine farm, producing award-winning vintages from vineyards rooted in these rolling hills. Visitors can enjoy wine tastings, harvest boards and tapas style dishes designed for sharing overlooking the vineyard.

Alongside there's also a hub for craft beer and artisanal brewing. From small-batch IPAs to farm-style ales, local microbreweries invite you to sample unique flavors in relaxed taprooms surrounded by green pastures and mountain air.

Take A Scenic Drive To Sani Pass & Lesotho

Rising dramatically through the Southern Drakensberg, the Sani Pass is one of the world’s most spectacular mountain routes, climbing from South Africa into the highlands of Lesotho.

Travel by 4x4 or on guided hikes along winding tracks with views of towering peaks, waterfalls, and valleys far below. At the summit, step into the Kingdom of Lesotho, a land rich in Basotho culture, traditional villages, and endless mountain panoramas. A stop at the legendary Sani Mountain Lodge—home to Africa’s highest pub—offers the perfect reward with hearty food and warm hospitality.

Read More

Swim the Midmar Mile at the Midmar Nature Reserve

Just outside Howick in the KwaZulu-Natal Midlands, Midmar Nature Reserve offers a blend of outdoor adventure and tranquil escapes along the shores of Midmar Dam.

Enjoy birdwatching, cycling, fishing, sailing, or simply relaxing by the water’s edge while surrounded by rolling hills and open skies. The reserve is best known as the home of the Midmar Mile, the world’s largest open-water swimming event, which attracts athletes and adventurers from across the globe each year.
